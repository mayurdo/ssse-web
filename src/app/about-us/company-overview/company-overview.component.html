More about our company…

Our Company, Shri Samarth Engineers Limited was incorporated 2019 by conversion of a partnership firm “M/s. Shri Swami Samarth Engineers (formerly known as M/s Swami Samarth Engineers Pvt Ltd)” into the Company as “M/s. Shri Swami Samarth Engineers Limited” under the provisions of Companies Act, 2013. are Class I-A Government Contractors with Public Works Department, Govt. of Maharashtra.

We work as Engineers, Contractors and are engaged in Development of Infrastructural projects like construction of Roads, Bridges, etc. Our Company is covering complete Civil Solutions in Irrigation for Government Projects.

We have been executing various Irrigation Projects viz; Dams, Barrages, Irrigation Tunnels, K.T. Weirs and Canals etc. developed for Govt. of Maharashtra.

We have over the years developed a reputation for delivering quality projects within the time stipulated in the contract. We were awarded a contract by the State of Maharashtra – PWD to construct a bridge across the Savitri River on the Mumbai Goa national highway that had collapsed in August, 2016. We were required under this contract to build a new bridge within a period of 180 days, which our Company successfully completed in a record time of 165 days . We also received a letter of appreciation from the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MORTH), Government of India for completing this project in record time
